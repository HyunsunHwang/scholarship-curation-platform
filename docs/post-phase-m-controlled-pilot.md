No outcome was inferred from missing output.

The expansion decision is HOLD because no expansion source produced a scholarship true positive. Attribution success alone cannot authorize expansion, production operation, or automatic publication.

The review lifecycle preserves the mistaken `needs_review` then `approve` events for `cau_003` and `cau_007` and adds superseding `reject` events. Effective decisions are reject, while the historical preview rows remain hidden with zero public leakage.

Runtime details are in `reports/post-phase-m-runtime-verification.json`, `reports/post-phase-m-source-health.json`, `reports/post-phase-m-semantic-reevaluation.json`, and the immutable cycle directories under `reports/post-phase-m-live`.
